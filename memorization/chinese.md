# Chinese Words

- `Hànyǔ`: Chinese language
- `Hànzì`: Chinese characters
- `Yīngyǔ`: English language
- `zàijiàn`: goodbye
- `nǐhǎo`: hello
- `Měiguó`: USA
- `Zhōngguó`: China
- `shuō`: speak
- `wǒ`: I; me
- `shì`: be
- `tā`: he
- `nǐ`: you
- `bù`: not
- `dōu`: all
- `xué`: learn
- `xǐhuan`: like
- `hē`: drink
- `mǎi`: buy
- `xiǎng`: want to

## Food

- `shuǐguǒ`: fruit
- `jīdàn`: egg
- `bǐnggān`: biscuit; cracker; cookie
- `miànbāo`: bread
- `mángguǒ`: mango
- `xīgua`: watermelon
- `bōluó`: pineapple
- `píngguǒ`: apple

## Drink

- `shuǐ`: water
